# Study Time Tracker 📊

A simple web application built using **Python and Flask** that helps students track their daily study hours and visualize them using charts.

This project helps students understand how much time they spend studying during the week and encourages better study habits.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students study throughout the week but do not clearly understand **how their study time is distributed across days**. This can lead to poor time management and inconsistent study routines.

---

## Solution

The **Study Time Tracker** allows users to enter their daily study hours for the week and generates a **visual chart** that shows how much time was spent studying each day.

This helps students analyze their study patterns and improve their productivity.

---

## Features

* Track study hours for each day of the week
* Generate a **bar chart visualization**
* Simple and clean interface
* Easy to use form input

---
